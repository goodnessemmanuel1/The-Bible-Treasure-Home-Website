About BTH

THE BIBLE TREASURE HOME (BTH) is a Christian ministry dedicated to spreading the Word of God across nations. Our mission is to bring the treasures of biblical wisdom to people from all walks of life, helping them discover the transformative power of God's Word.

Founded on the principles of faith, love, and service, BTH works tirelessly to create opportunities for spiritual growth and enlightenment. We believe that the Bible contains treasures of wisdom that can guide people through life's challenges and lead them to a deeper relationship with God.

Through various outreach programs, educational initiatives, daily bible studies, and community services, we strive to make the Word of God accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Vision

To create a world where every person has access to the life-changing message of the Bible, and where communities are transformed by the power of God's Word.

Our Core Values

Faith

We believe in the power of faith to transform lives and communities. Our work is grounded in unwavering faith in God and His Word.

Love

Love is at the heart of everything we do. We strive to demonstrate God's love through our actions, words, and service to others.

Service

We are committed to serving others with humility and compassion, following the example of Christ who came not to be served, but to serve.

Giving

We are committed to giving with humility and compassion, following the example of Christ, who came not to be served but to serve. Through generous giving whether of time, resources, or love we reflect His heart and extend His grace to those in need.

Bible Study

We are committed to the daily study of the word of God to be deeply rooted in His word. The word of God is the standard for our living.

Our Ministries

Bible Study Programs

Our comprehensive Bible study programs help individuals and groups deepen their understanding of Scripture and apply biblical principles to their daily lives.

Community Outreach

Through our community outreach initiatives, we bring the message of hope and salvation to underserved communities, providing both spiritual and material support.
