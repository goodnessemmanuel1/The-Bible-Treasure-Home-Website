Our Advisory Board

In addition to our executive team, THE BIBLE TREASURE HOME is guided by a distinguished advisory board comprising respected leaders in ministry, business, and community service. Their wisdom and counsel help shape our strategic direction and ensure that we remain faithful to our mission.

Board Responsibilities

  • Providing strategic guidance and oversight
  • Ensuring alignment with our mission and values
  • Advising on program development and expansion
  • Supporting fundraising and resource development
  • Representing BTH in the broader community
  • Planning and organizing BTH outreaches
