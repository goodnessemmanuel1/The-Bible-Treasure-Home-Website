Our Journey

Since our founding, THE BIBLE TREASURE HOME has been on a mission to spread the Word of God across nations. Our journey has been marked by growth, challenges, and countless testimonies of lives transformed by the power of God's Word. Here's a look at our progress over the years.

2020

Foundation of BTH

THE BIBLE TREASURE HOME was founded in the year 2020 and officially registered in the year 2023 with a vision to spread the Word of God across nations. Starting with just a small group of dedicated individuals, we began our journey of faith and service.

2021

First Outreach

We launched our first outreach program, reaching about 300 individuals with the message of hope and providing essential supplies to families in need.

2022

Year international expanion started

In the year 2022, by God’s grace, we began expanding internationally—spreading the message of Christ across borders, building connections with believers around the world, and establishing new avenues for ministry and outreach.

2023

Year That the Teens Club/Youth Ministry Was Lauched

In 2023 we witnessed the birth of our Teens Club, a vibrant youth ministry created to nurture the next generation in their walk with Christ, provide a space for fellowship, and ignite a passion for God among young hearts. We also so visited motherless homes in Nigeria and distributed bibles and welfare items

2024

Milestone Achievement

BTH conducted Bible and evangelical outreaches across the globe, reaching countless individuals and leading hundreds to Christ. By God's grace, we also distributed over 1000 copies of the Bible to those hungry for His Word.

2025

Started a Back To Scool Program

In January 2025, BTH launched the Back to School Program (BSP) to support children in their education. We visited numerous schools in rural areas across Nigeria, Pakistan, the United Kingdom, and Ghana. Through this initiative, we reached several communities, distributed over 1500 backpacks filled with study materials to children, and carried out community service projects in some of the urban areas where these schools are located.

Looking Ahead

As we look to the future, THE BIBLE TREASURE HOME remains committed to our mission of spreading the Word of God across nations. Our strategic plan for the coming years includes:

Expanded Reach

Extending our ministry to more countries, with a focus on regions where access to biblical resources is limited.

Digital Innovation

Developing new digital platforms and resources to make the Bible accessible to people of all ages and backgrounds.

Community Centers

Establishing community centers to serve as hubs for spiritual growth, education, and community support.
