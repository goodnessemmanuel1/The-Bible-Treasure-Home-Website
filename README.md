# The Bible Treasure Home — Website
 
> **Live site:** [thebibletreasurehome.com](https://thebibletreasurehome.com)
 
A professional multi-page website developed for **The Bible Treasure Home (BTH)**, a Christian ministry founded in 2020 and officially registered in 2023. The site serves as the organisation's primary digital presence — communicating its mission, showcasing its global leadership, and documenting its expanding impact across nations.
 
---
 
## About the Client
 
**The Bible Treasure Home (BTH)** is an international faith-based organisation dedicated to spreading the Word of God across nations. Its programmes and initiatives include:
 
- Daily Bible study programs
- Community outreach and evangelism
- Bible distribution (3,000+ copies distributed to date)
- Teens Club / Youth Ministry
- Back to School Program (launched January 2025)
- Widows support initiatives
- International representation across 6+ countries
 
**Founder:** Sister Joy Irefe-Esema
**Founded:** 2020 | **Registered:** 2023
**Headquarters:** Nigeria — with active representatives in the UK, Canada, Pakistan, Ghana, Côte d'Ivoire, and multiple Nigerian states

## Site Structure
 
```
/
├── index.html                  # Homepage
├── executives.html             # Leadership team
├── progress.html               # Ministry journey & milestones
├── donate.html                 # Support/donate page
├── images/                     # General site images (e.g. Golden Bible, Bible study)
├── community-outreach/         # Outreach event photos (recommend: rename from "community outreach")
└── executive-images/           # Leadership profile photos (recommend: rename from "executive images")
```
 
> **Note:** Current asset folder names contain spaces (`community outreach/`, `executive images/`) and some image paths use Windows-style backslashes (`\`). Renaming folders to hyphenated equivalents and updating paths to forward slashes (`/`) will improve cross-platform compatibility.
